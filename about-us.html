Who Are We

PetHealth Corporation is a non-profit organization dedicated to the protection of native Chinese dog breeds. Through advanced biological studies, the organization aims to enhance health monitoring systems, ensuring the well-being of pets across the region. By focusing on these native breeds, PetHealth Corporation seeks to improve the overall health of these dogs.

Our Services

To foster a healthy future for Chinese native dog breeds through dedicated research and health assessment programs aimed at ensuring their sustainable well-being.

Mobile Health Clinics for Native Breeds

Provide mobile health clinics that offer free health check-ups and vaccinations for native Chinese dog breeds in rural and underserved areas, ensuring their well-being and encouraging community engagement in breed conservation.

Virtual Canine Health Network

An online platform connecting dog owners with veterinary experts for real-time health advice and resources tailored to native Chinese dog breeds, fostering proactive care and enhancing breed-specific health outcomes.

PawPredict

A predictive health analytics platform utilizing AI to assess and preemptively address potential health issues in native Chinese dog breeds, fostering early intervention and ensuring long-term well-being.

Paws & People

These moments celebrate the special bond between humans and their furry companions. From shared walks to quiet connections, each photo tells a story of joy, trust, and the healing power of friendship.
